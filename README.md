## ✨ What this app does

## 🛠️ Using the application

The interface works in a simple way to help you manage your audio files.

### Starting a recording
Click the large red button to begin capturing audio. The app uses your default microphone. You will see a waveform appear on the screen as it detects sound. Click the same button to stop the recording when your meeting finishes.

### Processing your audio
The app saves your recording as a new project. Click the "Transcribe" button to start the text conversion. The software identifies who said what throughout the session. This process takes a few minutes depending on the length of your recording.

### Reading meeting notes
Once the transcription ends, you will see a text summary. The app highlights key points from your discussion. You can edit this text directly if you need to fix or add details.

### Asking questions
You can use the built-in search tool to ask questions about your projects. Type "What did we decide about the budget?" in the prompt box. The app scans your transcripts and gives you a direct answer. It cites the exact time in the audio file so you can verify the information yourself.

## 🔒 Your privacy

Most similar apps upload your audio to a server. H-VibeRec works differently. Every process happens inside your computer. Your microphone input, your audio files, and your transcripts never leave your hard drive. This ensures that sensitive business meetings or personal notes remain private. You do not need to create an account or provide an email address to use this software.

## ⚙️ Troubleshooting common issues

If you encounter problems, follow these checks to solve them:

*   **App does not open:** Ensure you have the latest updates installed for Windows. Restart your computer and try launching the app again.
*   **Audio sounds distorted:** Open your Windows sound settings and check the input volume for your microphone. Make sure you select the correct device in the H-VibeRec settings menu.
*   **Transcription feels slow:** Transcription requires significant computer power. Close other heavy programs like video editors or web browsers while the app works on a file.
*   **Missing text:** Keep the microphone close to the speakers to maintain clarity. Background noise can affect the accuracy of the transcription technology.
